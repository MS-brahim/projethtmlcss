Barber Shop

  Our services Our team is composed of talented and dynamic stylists.
Some have more experience and more training, while others have recently entered the profession.
To reflect this state of affairs, BraBarber has introduced a level system, reflecting the experience and breadth of each person's know-how.
A higher level means a more accomplished stylist with more experience.
Its versatility and know-how make it a hairdresser more in demand: its prices will be higher than a lower level stylist.
Levels are absolutely not a measure of competence: all members of the team are trained in the rules of art.
However, some stylists are more in demand and by their experience, bring this little extra to their art, distinguishing themselves from others.
Consult our price list by stylist and / or level.
